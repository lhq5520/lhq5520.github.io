About Hengyue Zhai

关于翟恒越

He was someone who lived life with an undeniable intensity, always pushing the limits and embracing whatever adventure came his way. We shared countless moments, often with the roar of an engine in the background, our shared love for cars fueling some of our wildest days. His knowledge of cars was unmatched, and his excitement when talking about them was contagious. Whether it was a drive or a late-night conversation, his passion was always evident.

Juice WRLD was always in the mix, the soundtrack to our bold, carefree escapades, and it reflected his love for hip-hop and the deeper emotions he often kept locked inside. He wasn't just someone who loved fast cars and good music; he had an adventurous spirit and a loyalty that made him the kind of friend you could always count on.

But beneath that fearless exterior, he was quietly facing battles we couldn't see. For as long as we knew him, none of us realized the weight he was carrying until it was too late. He fought his struggles in silence, and his loss has left a void that words can't fill. Still, I'll always hold on to the memories we made—the moments where he shined the brightest, even when he was fighting the hardest battle within.

他是一个活得无比疯狂的人， 总是勇敢地突破极限，迎接每一次冒险的挑战。 我们一起度过了无数难忘的时光，汽车是我们共同的激情，它点燃了过去那些疯狂的日子。 他对车的热爱无人能及，每次聊起汽车，他的兴奋的感觉总是记忆犹新。 无论是激情澎湃的激烈驾驶还是深夜的静谧巡航，我们总是能在车上找到自我。

Juice WRLD的音乐总是伴随着我们深夜巡航的旅途， 它不仅体现了他对嘻哈音乐的热爱，也反映了他内心深处复杂的情感。他不止是个热爱音乐和冒险的人， 还是一个忠实可靠的朋友，总能在关键时刻让你依赖和信任。

但是，在他无畏的外表下，他悄悄地面对着我们看不见的战斗。 尽管我们认识了他很久，但我们谁也没意识到他肩负的重担，直到为时已晚。 他在沉默中与自己的挣扎抗争，他的离去留下了一个无法用语言填补的空白。 不过，我会一直珍藏那些我们共同创造的回忆。

最后，天堂没有痛苦，你在我们心里永远活在24岁。永远年轻，充满活力。
